kNOw Plastics

Educational multiplayer online game

kNOwPLASTICS Online Game is a multi-player memory game. It is based on Memory, also known as Concentration, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, or Pairs, where the objective is to find matching pairs of cards. We developed the game engine and the in-browser app.

WasteLess India is an non-profit social enterprise, based in Auroville, South India. Their mission is to transform the way we think about waste by promoting responsible waste management and environmental sustainability. They developed an educational program kNOw PLASTICS which includes a memory game. Teachers can use the multi-week program to educate young students (6-12 years) why it is important to reduce the use of unsafe and single-use plastics. Their kNOwPLASTICS program
