Solar Shop

Online shop for Auroville Energy Products.

The online Solar Shop offers a range of equipment for generating solar energy. Built on the PrestaShop platform, the website allows regular customers to effortlessly purchase products with minimal human intervention. The registration and checkout process is user-friendly for both returning and new customers.

Auroville Energy Products is an organization that sells equipment for renewable energy to customers all over India. This includes solar panels, batteries, solar chargers, inverters, and all the accessories needed to set up a complete solar energy generating system. Their customers are mainly dealers and installers, but some end-users also buy equipment for their private installations. Dealers can receive discounts if they sign up with their company details and demonstrate sufficient knowledge to become resellers. To help them, Auroville Energy Products provides training for new dealers and their employees.

Implementation

The open-source project PrestaShop was used as the basis for Solar Shop . A theme and add-on modules were built to customize it. Special attention was given to Search Engine Optimization, including the addition of meta-tags to the theme, allowing Google to scan for product information. This resulted in a perfect score in the Indian Google search results. When Indian customers searched for applicable products, Solar Shop was always the top purchasing option.

I developed a module for payment options and created a custom shipping module that calculates shipping costs based on the weight of the purchased products.

About PrestaShop

PrestaShop is user-friendly and versatile. It offers a wide range of features, including:

  • Stock Management: Easily track inventory and manage stock levels.
  • Taxes: Configure tax rules and apply them automatically based on location and product type.
  • Languages: Support for multiple languages to cater to a global audience.
  • Currency: Handle transactions in various currencies with real-time exchange rate updates.
  • Customer Relations: Tools for managing customer accounts, communication, and support.

Overall, it provides a comprehensive solution for running an online store with efficiency and ease.

Technology

PrestaShop, PHP, CSS, HTML, Google Analytics, Google Search Console
