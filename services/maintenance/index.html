Maintenance & Support

Top-tier computer services now available on Saba

Are you on Saba and in need of top-tier computer maintenance and support? Look no further! With roots dating back to the early 1990s in the Netherlands, our expertise spans decades.

Originally, we specialized in selling and servicing Apple computers and peripherals for printing companies and graphic artists. We offered comprehensive maintenance, color correction for screens and printers, backup system advice, and software training, primarily for Adobe and Quark (we have worked with PhotoShop 2.0, Illustrator 3.0). Later, we gained experience in Linux and Microsoft Windows operating systems.

Today, we’re proud to bring this wealth of experience to the beautiful island of Saba. Trust us to keep your systems running smoothly with the same dedication and quality service that has been our hallmark since day one. We can solve your network problems, install software and operating systems, and set up WiFi. Contact us for reliable computer maintenance and support.
