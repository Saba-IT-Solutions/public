Hosting

Hosting of websites, webshops, ERP, etc.

Choosing a hosting provider for your website or shared application can be overwhelming. With so many factors to consider, it’s not always easy to navigate. I can help you choose a solution tailored to your needs.

Shared Hosting

A shared hosting environment is ideal for websites with a moderate number of daily visitors. This solution is particularly suitable for small to medium-sized businesses, providing a cost-effective and reliable option.

Virtual Private Server (VPS)

A Virtual Private Server (VPS) may be an good choice when a bit more processing power is required. As the business expands and the number of visitors increases, a VPS can scale alongside, ensuring the web applications perform optimally.

Mail Services

SabaITSolutions offers a variety of email solutions to meet your needs. You can use a free service from a renowned provider to add your email addresses, or SabaITSolutions can set up a self-hosted VPS for you. Both solutions have their own pros and cons.
