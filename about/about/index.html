About

Friday, Dec 20, 2024

John Zandbergen

My name is John. I am Dutch and from the east of the Netherlands. A very long time ago, I studied Computer Science in Enschede. Shortly after obtaining my Bachelor’s degree, I started a computer company where our main clients were graphic artists, designers, and offset printers. We sold, installed, and maintained Apple computers, peripherals, and networks. I also created customized software for various customers, gradually including more and more websites.

In the first decade of the 2000s, I shifted more to programming and almost exclusively worked as freelancer at larger software companies. I built websites, web applications, and desktop applications in several Microsoft .NET programming languages.

In 2010, my partner and I moved to India, where I worked at several places, ranging from management assistant at a vocational training institute to director of a small organization dealing in renewable energy components. My programming work continued and focused on websites for Indian organizations, but I also built a tool for a Dutch architect to help potential new homeowners budget their building projects.

Privately, we were ready to leave India and considered moving back to the Netherlands. However, my partner found work on Saba, so we ended up on the Unspoiled Queen of the Caribbean. We are happy to be here, and I’m excited to offer my skills and knowledge through this new endeavor, Saba IT Solutions.
